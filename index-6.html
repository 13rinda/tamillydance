Hire a Belly Dancer

for the ‘WOW’ ✨ factor at your event? Why not hire Tamilly to add that shimmy and sparkle you’re looking for?

Tamilly is a professional belly dancer who can perform in various styles, excelling in authentic and modern belly dancing. Whether you are looking for a belly dance performance that once was seen in the Egyptian Golden Age Era, or something more up-to-date, Tamilly can tailor the dancing to meet your needs. If you’re looking for specific props, styles, or multiple dancers, Tamilly can arrange this for you too.

Types of events include corporate, weddings, birthdays, hen parties, Christmas parties, restaurants, and stage performances, etc.

For all enquiries please fill out the form.

For a quote please include the date and nature of the event, venue address including postcode, estimated start time, how long you would like the performance to last, as well as number of attendees, and any additional preferences such as favourite songs, costume colour preference, etc.

Please give as much information as possible for a spectacular event.

Please note that booking must be made in full payment and are non-refundable. If the required payment is not received within one week of invoicing, the booking will no longer be valid. Depending on the event's location and time, additional travel costs may be necessary.
