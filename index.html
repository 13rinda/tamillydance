LOOKING FOR A BELLY DANCER?

Tamilly is a professional belly dancer and dance teacher who regularly performs for all events. She has performed at various high-profile events, from weddings to birthday parties and balls. Tamilly started dancing at the esteemed Fleur Estelle Dance School and studied and performed in Egypt and Spain. She is also the founder and president of the Cambridge University Belly Dance Society, where she teaches members regular beginner belly dance classes. No event is too big or small for Tamilly, and she is ready to add the sparkle to your event.
