about

Dee Founder of Tamilly

I’m Dee, founder of Tamilly Dance, and I’ve been belly dancing for over 7 years. I started dancing at a very low point in my life. I had experienced injustice and didn’t see the point in living. I wanted to find something to distract me, apart from my job. I’ve been wanting to dance for a long time but was scared. It was out of my comfort zone, but I gave myself one last chance. I went to my first class thinking that this wasn’t for me and that I would quit. But I was left surprised. As soon as I stepped into my first class, I was hooked. The smile I hadn’t seen in a very long time returned, and I felt happy again. It was as if my inner child had woken up. Belly dance had woken up something I didn’t know existed inside me. I wanted more of it and made it my mission to take as many classes as possible. I took part in Fleur Estelle Dance School classes and performed in the termly showcases. I watched myself grow confident and become more comfortable in my skin. The more I danced, the more my self-esteem increased. I was so in love and still in love with the beautiful dancing that I decided to train further with Amora Shams in Spain and Egypt, gaining a UNESCO Level 3 Qualification in Belly Dance. Fast forward to today as a full circle moment, I am now part of the Fleur Estelle Dance Company’s troupe and perform solo gigs, too. I hope to share the joy of belly dancing with you and bring sparkles to your event.
